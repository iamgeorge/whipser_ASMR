A product that allows users to generate their preferred music and images by AI, creating a surface with visual and auditory elements, then help user improve their focus and positive emotion in learning or working.

Why music and visual?

  • Audio can be a powerful tool for affecting memory, emotional states, and learning outcomes (Akhshabi & Dortaj, 2023; Angwin et al., 2017; Gallego-Gómez et al., 2020; Jakubowski & Ghosh, 2019; Söderlund et al., 2021).
    • Auditory stimuli, particularly music, impact cognitive processes, which can reduce stress and improve academic performance.
    • Music can also be a therapy that can positively influence emotional states, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being.
  • Learners who engage with visual content that aligns with their personal preferences often experience improved attention and retention.
    • When students are presented with materials that match their aesthetic or visual preferences, they are more likely to be motivated and engaged, enhancing learning outcomes (Harris & J. McDonald, 2016).

However, barely any product tries to combine personalized music with images to create a user preference learning environment.



Highlights

Visual

AI-generated personalized images based on user preferences are set as the desktop wallpaper. These images will be used to enhance emotional engagement and focus.

Auditory

AI-generated background music (BGM) and white noise tailored to the generated image. Users can select from various music types, including white noise, ambient music, or combined sounds, creating a personalized auditory environment.

Personalized

Text/draw input to generate visual content and the ability to control music preferences (type, duration, intensity) that align with the generated visual.
